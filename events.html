AIGA Gala

Mark your calendars because it's that time of the year again- time to buy your tickets to the annual AIGA Gala! The gala will take place the evening of April 15th at Pier Sixty, 23rd street and the West Side Highway in New York City. This year brings Dana Arnett from SVA and Su Matthews Hale of Lippincott as the chairs of the event. We'll be celebrating the distinguished practitioners in the field of design, whether they be professionals in the field or teachers working to shape the next generation of designers.

Spaces Launch Party

The creative firm Spaces recently opened up a new office in Sydney, Australia, and what better way to start than with a party. The launch party for Spaces was a success and bodes well for the future of the branch. Guest speakers like Dr. Willem Cosijn, the Consul General for The Netherlands, gave speeches as guests dined on local oysters and ice cream.
